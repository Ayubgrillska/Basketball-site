How to dribble

when dribling there is different types of dribbles to use to get by diffenders. Allowing players to move the ball around the court, evade defenders, and create scoring opportunities. Mastery of dribbling enhances your control over the game, making you a more versatile and effective player.

The Behind the back dribble

This drible is used to switch hands and change direction while keeping the ball away from a defender. Move the ball behind your back from one hand to the other. This requires a smooth and controlled motion, ensuring the ball doesn’t go too far from your body.

The crossover dribble

The crossover is used to quickly change direction and evade a defender.Dribble the ball from one hand to the other in a swift, low motion. Use a quick lateral step to shift your body in the direction of the new dribble.

The Between The Legs dribble

Between the leg dribble i used to change direction and protect the ball from a defender. Dribble the ball between your legs from one hand to the other. Step forward or backward with the opposite foot to create space and maintain balance.
