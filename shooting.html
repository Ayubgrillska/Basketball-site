-IMPORTANCE OF SHOOTING-

Keep your feet no further than shoulder width apart, preferably closer together, and slightly staggered.

Grip the ball correctly. Position your elbow so it's inline with your shoulder, not directly under the ball

Push the ball upward with your shooting hand and Release the ball.

Practice shooting as much as you can, so that taking a shot - from the stance and grip to the jump and release - feels natural.
